Help

The Training Dashboard Website is an extension of the Troop 1094 website. It has two main pages: the dashboard and the calendar, each of their functions displayed below.

Dashboard

The dashboard has a row for each adult with their name, training expiration date, email address, and any recent events they are registered for. The urgency of an adult's training (how soon it could affect their chaperoning events) is color coded in the following method:

Red (!)

Chaperone has a registered event that they will not be able to go to because it is past their training expiry date with priority on people who's training has not yet expired (will appear toward the top of the color section in the table).

Yellow (?):

Chaperone is within two months of expiry or within six months of past expiry of training with priority for people with registered events (will appear toward the top of the color section in the table).

Green (âœ“):

Adult has an active training (completed and not within two months of expiration) and can have both a registered event or none upcoming.

Gray

Chaperone is past six months from previous expiry and has not renewed training.
